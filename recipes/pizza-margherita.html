Pizza Margherita Recipe

Description:

Margherita pizza is a beloved Italian classic, featuring a thin, golden crust topped with a vibrant tomato sauce,
melted mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves. The simple combination of ingredients creates a harmony of flavors,
where the tangy sauce complements the creamy cheese, and the aromatic basil adds a fresh, herbaceous note.
Baked until the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned, this pizza offers a satisfying blend of textures and tastes that
exemplify the beauty of traditional Italian cuisine.

ingredients:

For the Dough:

  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 1 ½ cups warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup homemade or store-bought pizza sauce
  • 2 cups fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1 ½ cups fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until frothy.
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil.
    • Mix until the dough starts to come together, then knead on a floured surface for about 8-10 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.
  2. Preheat the Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to its highest temperature (usually around 475°F/245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
  3. Shape the Dough:
    • Punch down the dough to release air, then divide it into two equal portions. Roll each portion into a ball.
    • On a floured surface, stretch and roll out the dough into a circle about 12 inches in diameter. Transfer the dough to a pizza peel or baking sheet dusted with flour or cornmeal.
  4. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
    • Arrange the mozzarella slices evenly over the sauce. Scatter fresh basil leaves on top.
    • Drizzle the pizza with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake:
    • Slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  6. Serve:
    • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. For an extra touch, sprinkle with grated Parmesan and a few more fresh basil leaves.
